Welcome to the PanGenome Creator

The Prokaryotic Genome

The prokaryotic genome is one continuous strand of DNA, unlike ours, which is made of multiple chromosomes. Because it is circular, the prokaryotic genome is perfect for CIRCOS visualization software, which, for this program, shows the overlap of the genomes of many different species.

Genomic Comparision

One reason this is useful is for inter-species genomic comparision. If you have many similar species, it may be useful to pinpoint specific genes that are common to a group as well as isolate genes that are unique to a specific strand. In this way, you may be able to see how a specific gene contributes to that species' environment.
